Bangladesh, my beloved nation, has made remarkable progress in many areas, including poverty alleviation, women's empowerment, and digital innovation. However, challenges persist. Economic growth faces pressure from rising inequality and unemployment, especially among the youth. Climate change poses a significant threat to our future, with floods and rising sea levels affecting millions. Political polarization hinders progress, and corruption remains a barrier to equitable development. Yet, I see immense potential in our people, particularly in young entrepreneurs. We must foster innovation, support social business, and focus on sustainable development to secure a brighter future. By investing in education, health, and clean energy, Bangladesh can achieve inclusive growth and inspire the world with its resilience and vision.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
